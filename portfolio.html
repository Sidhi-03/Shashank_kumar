01 — About

Eye behind
the lens

I'm a cinematographer focused on visual storytelling, combining technical expertise in camera systems, lens selection, and lighting design with a strong cinematic sensibility. Experienced in collaborating with agencies and creative teams to deliver high-quality, emotionally driven visuals, overseeing projects from concept to final frame with precision and artistic intent.

I approach cinematography as a craft where emotion, environment, and narrative intersect through the lens. Each frame I create is guided by lived experiences and a commitment to visual authenticity. My work focuses on translating story into immersive imagery that connects audiences to both reality and artistry.

Education: Completed a Diploma in Cinematography from Mindscreen Film Institute, specializing in camera operations and advanced lighting techniques (structured day and night setups). Graduated with distinction — ranked among the top three candidates during admission.
