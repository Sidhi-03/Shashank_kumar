01 — About

Eye behind
the lens

I'm a 21-year-old filmmaker and cinematographer based in Hyderabad, currently working as an Assistant Director on Godachari 2 — a high-profile Telugu spy-action thriller with People Media Factory.

My work spans costume coordination, artist-calling AD work, and AI-driven VFX concept visualization — bridging the traditional set and the future of production.

Ultimately, I want to direct and shoot films with the visual literacy of a DP and the storytelling instincts of a director. Influenced by Cuarón, Spielberg, and Miyazaki.
