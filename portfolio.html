About Me

I'm a cinematographer focused on visual storytelling, combining technical expertise in camera systems, lens selection, and lighting design with a strong cinematic sensibility.

I approach cinematography as a craft where emotion, environment, and narrative intersect through the lens. Each frame I create is guided by lived experiences and a commitment to visual authenticity.

Completed a Diploma in Cinematography from Mindscreen Film Institute, specializing in camera operations and advanced lighting techniques. Graduated with distinction — ranked among the top three candidates during admission.
